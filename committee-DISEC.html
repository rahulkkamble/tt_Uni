DISEC

Disarmament and International Security Committee

Agenda

Assessing the implications of the Sudanese Civil War

Description

The Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC) is The First Committee of the United Nations General Assembly, and deals with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affect the international community and look for solutions to the challenges in the international security regime. Its main aim is to minimize threats posed by weapons, especially weapons of mass destruction, including chemical, biological, and nuclear weapons. DISEC also focuses on issues pertaining to conventional weapons, new military technologies, terrorism, cyber war, and weaponization of new areas, including outer space.

Letter from the Chair

Dear Delegates,

Hello everyone! My name is Orhan Khan, and I’m excited to be serving as the Chairperson of the Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC). This committee tackles some of the most important global issues related to peace, security, and disarmament, and I’m looking forward to seeing the unique perspectives each of you brings to the table. As Chair, my goal is to make DISEC a space where delegates feel comfortable speaking up, asking questions, and engaging in meaningful debate. Whether you’re an experienced MUNner or attending your first conference, I encourage you to participate actively, think critically, and enjoy the process of diplomacy.

I’m here to guide discussions, ensure smooth debate, and help you make the most out of this committee experience. I can’t wait to see the ideas, creativity, and collaboration that will come out of our sessions. Wishing you all an engaging and memorable DISEC experience and best of luck, and see you in committee!

Dhriyaan Rajesh

Co-Secretary General

Letter from the Co-Chair

Dear Delegates,

I’m Dhriyaan, and I’ll be serving as your Co-Chair for the Disarmament and International Security Committee.

I chose DISEC because it deals with issues that actually shape how safe or unsafe the world is. The decisions discussed here are serious, and I want this committee to reflect that, while still being a space where everyone feels comfortable speaking, even beginners.

As a person who has been a beginner and a newbie, I will make sure each and every one of you speaks.

As Co-Chair, my goal is to keep debate fair, focused, and respectful. I appreciate delegates who come prepared, speak clearly, and are open to listening to others. You do not need to be perfect, but you do need to be engaged.

I’m looking forward to working with all of you and seeing how this committee develops over the session.

Thank you.
